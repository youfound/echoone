Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and enhance our services. The information we collect falls into the following categories:

1. User-Provided Information

This includes data you provide when interacting with our Website. For instance, when creating an account, using our services, making financial transactions, engaging on social media, providing feedback, or communicating with us. 2. Website Usage Information

We automatically collect data related to your usage of our Website. This comprises information such as geo-location data, IP addresses, device details, browser specifications, and web log data.

3. Third-Party Data

When you interact with us through third-party platforms (e.g., social media or mobile applications), we may access specific information from your profiles, in accordance with your privacy settings on those platforms.

4. Payment Information

We do not directly collect payment card details. Payments are securely processed by reputable third-party payment gateway service providers.
